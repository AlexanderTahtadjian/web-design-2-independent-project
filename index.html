What is "Elementals"

"Elementals" is an adventure based story set in the sci-fi/fantasy world of Arlos where we get to see things in a way that we've never seen them before. Accepetance, belief, faith, destiny, choice, mythology, quests, prophecies, unity and freedom are just some of the themes that this story focuses on and truly make the reader feeel like their journying with the characters of the story.

What is the story of "Elementals"

The realm of Arlos has always had a history of being at the center of world changing events throughout it's entire history. For thousands of years, everyone has had a prosperous ;life in this era of peace with the elements in perfect balance and harmony, but some things never last forever. With war on the horizon, and a dark force returning from the ancient past, acompany of mismatched adventures, must band together to complete a quest, and search for ancient relics to help save their world and restore balace. Join our brave guardians as they travel through magical lands, mysterious forests and unknown regions to fulfill their destinies.
